The creative thinking behind it

  • One honest gate. The card unlocks on exactly the signal that means "you're done" — the Scorecard being READY — so a learner never sees a certificate they can't open, or a lock after they've finished.
  • Mobile is the main screen. The full landscape certificate scales to fit any phone with nothing clipped; the download is a single sticky button that produces a clean A4 PDF.
  • Earned, not transactional. The unlock is a small celebration — a "Ready" pill, a warm sheet, the child's name — not a paywall or an upsell.
  • Trustworthy by design. Every certificate carries a QR to a public verify page proving it's genuine; the credential ID is random and unguessable, the page is noindexed, and an erased child's name is never served (DPDPA).
  • Whole across the range. The same flow serves all 7 streams and all three tiers — the certificate reads AACE 5, 15 or 64 from the learner's own challenge.
