Parent consent screen — wording, before & after

The screen a parent sees before their child's Success Scan. Same one tick, same one button — the words get clearer and honest to the law.

For sign-off · draft · not live to children until approved

Why change it

Today the parent ticks a single line that bundles two different things — “agree to the Terms” (a contract) and “approve for my child” (data consent) — and it says almost nothing about what they're approving. The law wants the child-data consent to stand on its own and to actually state what it covers. So we split the tick and restore the fuller (but still plain) wording. The parent still does one tick and one button — the flow doesn't get longer.

Step 2 · Your permission for Aarav
Your permission

This is only about using Aarav's Success Scan — separate from the Terms you already accepted.

I am Aarav's parent or guardian, and I'm agreeing on their behalf.

I allow iRisePro to use Aarav's answers in the Success Scan to work out their abilities and create their personal report and suggestions.

  • This is my choice — I don't have to agree.
  • I can change my mind and withdraw any time; then you stop using Aarav's data for this.
  • It won't be held against Aarav, and won't affect anything already done.
  • You'll never use Aarav's data for ads, tracking, or profiling.
