iRisePro Declaration: Analysis of the AACE64™© Scorecard

This AACE64™© Success Scorecard reflects the student's current Success Abilities profile based on demonstrated performance during the 90-Minute iRisePro All-India ASLI Championship. The pillar percentages, benchmark comparisons and insights represent the student's present alignment with the All-India Success Abilities Benchmarks (3 Tiers).

This Framework measures foundational Success Abilities across the four AACE64™© pillars—Attitude, Aptitude, Communication and Execution—which play a significant role in academic performance, learning effectiveness and long-term success. These abilities influence how students learn, retain information, solve problems, communicate ideas and apply themselves consistently in academic settings.

As students continue to grow and engage in future developmental activities, the complexity of challenges, performance expectations and benchmark standards may evolve. Accordingly, AACE64™© pillar scores and ability profiles may change over time as students develop stronger Success Abilities through learning, practice and experience.
