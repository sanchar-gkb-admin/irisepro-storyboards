Our thinking

The current copy is accurate, but it's written from the system's point of view — section labels ("Part A", "Benchmark Gap Analysis"), branded jargon, and "The student" instead of the child's name. It reads like a report generator.

The editorial voice keeps every fact, number, claim, benchmark, plan and the Declaration exactly as-is — it only changes the framing: parent-facing headings, the child's name, and a plain-language handle for the jargon. Nothing scientific is reworded or reinvented.

We're not asking you to accept it — we're asking you to choose, heading by heading if you like.
